APACHE RTR 200 4V

TVS Apache RTR 200’s advanced 197.75cc, four valve racing O3C oil- cooled oversquare, five-speed engine is equipped with balancer shaft mechanism and is programmed to produce 20.2 bhp @8000 rpm with a torque of 18.1 Nm @ 7000 rpm. For the first time in the 200cc segment, TVS has introduced an oil cooled combustion chamber with ram air assist, which enables the motorcycle to achieve a top speed of 128 km/h with a performance of 0-60km/h in just 3.9 seconds. >
